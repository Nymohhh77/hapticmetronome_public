# WatchHapticMetronome Privacy Policy

At WatchHapticMetronome, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data (or rather, how we don’t) to provide a secure, lightweight, and private experience.

*Effective Date: March 09, 2026*

## Our Commitment to Privacy

WatchHapticMetronome is built with a **zero data collection** philosophy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Our goal is to provide an entertainemnt tool, secure, and private without tracking you or your activities.

## What Data Do We Collect?

**We collect no data.** WatchHapticMetronome operates entirely on your device, processing data locally. Here’s what this means:

- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any other identifiable information.
- **No Logs**: Your logs are not stored or transmitted to any server.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use analytics, cookies, or any form of tracking to monitor your activity.
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: Since we collect no data, there’s nothing to share with third parties.
